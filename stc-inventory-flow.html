Department 1 — Procurement
Adding Products & Purchasing Stock
This department is responsible for two things: first, registering new products into the system catalog; second, creating purchases (Adhoc) that bring stock into the warehouse. Everything in the system starts here.
1A
Add Products to Catalog
Register a new item so it can be purchased and tracked
⌄
💡
Before any purchase can happen, the product must exist in the system. This is like creating a product profile — name, category, what unit it's measured in. Once added, it can be bought, stored, and dispatched.

1B
Create Purchase — Add to Adhoc Stock
Buy the product from a supplier and bring it into warehouse stock
⌄
📦
A purchase (called an Adhoc in this system) records what was bought, from whom, at what price, and how many units arrived. This is the moment stock enters the warehouse. Each purchase creates an Adhoc PPA (Purchase Advice) record that tracks that batch of stock separately.

Department 2 — Warehouse
Rack Management & Receiving Products
After Procurement buys the stock, Warehouse takes over. They organize products into racks, update stock balances, and confirm physical receipt of any goods that arrive at the store.
2A
Update Rack for Product
Assign purchased stock to a physical rack location
⌄
🗄️
Every item in the warehouse sits in a named Rack. When a new purchase arrives, warehouse staff assign it to a rack. The rack name is recorded against the Adhoc PPA so that when dispatching later, staff know exactly where to pick from.
Rack update fields

Which item is being stored

Physical storage location

Balance Quantity
Units available in this rack
📌
Important: When balance reaches 0, the rack row is automatically removed from the active dispatch list. Warehouse must keep this updated.

✅

Department 3 — Dispatch
Check Pending Orders & Dispatch Stock
This department receives order requests from sites and customers. They check what's needed, verify if stock is available in the warehouse, then either dispatch immediately or route back to Procurement if stock is low.
3A
Check Pending List from Sites & Customers
View all open requisitions waiting to be fulfilled
⌄
📋
Sites raise requisitions (material requests). These appear in the Dispatch department's pending list. Each line shows what's needed, how much, and how urgently — urgent rows are highlighted in red.

↓
3B
Check Stock Availability
Is there enough in the rack to fulfil the order?
⌄
🔍
For each pending item, Dispatch checks the Adhoc rack balance. They link the requested item to the matching product in the warehouse catalog, then see how much is available. The result determines what happens next.
Is stock available in rack?
✅
Yes — Stock Available
→ Go to Step 3C: Dispatch
Select the Adhoc PPA, enter the dispatch quantity, and confirm. Balance reduces automatically.
⚠️
No — Insufficient Stock
→ Route to Department 1
Mark the item as Pending with a remark. Send a purchase request to Procurement (Dept 1) to buy more stock.

Department 4 — Delivery
Customer Receipt, Challan & Closing Outlets
After dispatch, this department ensures goods reach the customer with a proper challan (delivery document), confirms receipt, and closes out the order once everything is accounted for — either received or transferred.
4A
Generate Delivery Challan
Create the physical delivery document that travels with the goods
⌄
📄
A challan is the delivery slip that physically accompanies the goods. It lists what was dispatched, to whom, from which warehouse batch (PPA), and must be signed off when received. Once verified, quantities cannot be edited.

4B
Customer Receives or Transfers
Goods delivered to site, or transferred to another location
⌄
🤝
When the goods arrive, the customer/supervisor either receives them directly (confirms qty on site) or they may be transferred to another location via a Transfer Challan. Both outcomes must be recorded to close the order.
📬
Direct Receipt
Supervisor confirms at site
Quantity received is entered. Status changes to Received. Ledger updated.
🔄
Transfer Challan
Moved to another location
A Transfer Challan is generated with product, qty, source, and destination. Verify Dispatch confirms receipt at new location.

⌄

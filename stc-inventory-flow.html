Inventory Management

Adhoc & Special Item Tracking

The Adhoc warehouse is the central inventory from which all dispatches originate. Special item types have dedicated tracking layers beyond standard stock movements.

AD

Adhoc → Rack → Dispatch → Track

Rack Management · Tools Track (PPA) · PPE Tracker · RCM Transfer Challan

🗄️ Rack Management
Adhoc stock is physically stored in named Racks. When dispatching, GLD staff select the specific Adhoc PPA (Purchase Advice) and rack. Each PPA tracks a balance quantity — when it reaches 0, it is removed from the active dispatch list. Rack names appear in dispatch records for full traceability.
🔧 Tools Track (PPA)
For Tools & Tackles items, each purchased tool gets a unique serial/asset ID (PPA tool row). After dispatching from Adhoc, GLD staff select the specific tool from the dropdown and click Send — creating a record in stc_tooldetails_track that links the tool to the exact requisition and site it was sent to.

Prerequisite: Tool rows must be created at purchase time. Without them, dispatch cannot be tracked.
🦺 PPE Notes & Tracker
PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) items follow a similar pattern. At dispatch, a PPE Note records: item, quantity issued, recipient (supervisor/site), date, and Adhoc PPA reference. The PPE Tracker maintains a running log for audits and per-site balance checks.
📄 RCM Transfer Challan
For internal stock movements, an RCM Transfer Challan is generated — covering the product code, description, quantity, unit, Adhoc PPA reference, and authorized signatory. Physical goods move with this challan. At the destination, Verify Dispatch marks it as confirmed and updates the ledger (stock out against PPA).

⚠️ Exception Paths
🚫 Manager Rejection (Status 6)
Item is rejected with a mandatory reason. Supervisor is informed. End of journey for this line.
🚫 Procurement Rejection (Status 6)
Even after manager approval, Procurement can reject at their stage with a reason. End of journey.
⏳ GLD Pending (Status 9)
Adhoc stock insufficient. GLD marks pending with a remark. Resumes when stock is replenished.
↩️ Supervisor Return (Status 8)
After receipt, supervisor can return the item. Marked Returned in the ledger.
